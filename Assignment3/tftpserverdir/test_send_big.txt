One day something happened which in a roundabout way was enlightening. It was a tiny incident in itself, 
but it gave me a better glimpse than I had had before of the real nature of imperialism – the real motives for which despotic governments act. 
Early one morning the sub-inspector at a police station the other end of the town rang me up on the phone, and said that an elephant was ravaging the bazaar.
